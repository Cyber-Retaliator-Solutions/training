IBM Analytics

IBM Analytics is a term that refers to the products, services, and solutions that IBM offers to help organizations collect, analyze, and use data for various purposes, such as business intelligence, decision making, performance management, predictive analytics, and artificial intelligence. IBM Cognos Analytics with Watson is a web-based integrated business intelligence suite by IBM. IBM Analytics Training allows your business to uncover opportunities in all types of data from a variety of sources, within and beyond your organization. The training in this aspect will help to turn insight gained from data into action more efficiently. IBM analytics has a diverse set of solutions. Training Modalities

Instructor-led Online (ILO)

Instructor-led training with the convenience of having training delivered directly to your PC, with live instructions. Instructor-led Online Live virtual courses are delivered over the web using services such as VOIP technology and hands-on labs, without the need to travel. It is ideal for a globally dispersed audience or anyone working or studying from home.

Instructor-led (Classroom)

This is traditional classroom-based teaching where lessons are held at a set time with other students. The instructor leads the class, motivating and guiding the students, with slides and hands-on Labs whilst establishing a friendly and safe learning environment ideal for getting certified ready and gaining practical experience.

Web-Based Training (WBT)

A modularly designed mix of Multimedia interactive labs and course material. These are presented in an online course format.

Self-Paced Virtual Classes (SPVC)

IBM's Self-Paced Virtual Classes are flexible training option that allows students to master skills via online courses that fit their schedules. Courses typically take between 1 and 5 days to complete, however, upon activation, courses are accessible for a 30-day period via online reading assignments, multimedia presentations, and demonstrations.
